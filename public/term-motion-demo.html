What to look for. In natural mode the moving term is one persistent element: it stays opaque, travels in a slight arc, the sign flips the instant it crosses =, and only afterwards do neighbors reflow. In crossfade mode every delta plays at once with opacity blending — freeze it in slow-mo and you'll see double-exposure ghosting, the thing to reject in review.
